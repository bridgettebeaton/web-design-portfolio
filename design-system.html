Design Decisions

I use a clean sans-serif font for readability on screens. I created a consistent type scale so headings and body text stay balanced across pages. Body text is 16px with comfortable line spacing. I use one primary blue (#0E4C67) for navigation, headings, links, and buttons to maintain consistency. Dark gray text on white ensures strong contrast, and links are underlined to clearly show interactivity.
